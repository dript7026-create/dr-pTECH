# Frequency-Based Reality Simulation in DoENGINE

- All systems, entities, and events operate on frequency, motion, and vibration principles.
- The engine pulse (tick) is a frequency; every Doer, Motif, and Vibe is modeled as a waveform.
- Reality is simulated as a dynamic field of interacting frequencies, with resonance and interference patterns.
- Encryption, metadata, and hardware traces are all frequency-modulated for security and uniqueness.
- This approach enables adaptive, emergent, and future-proof simulation and security.
