# DirkOdds Android Executive Summary

DirkOdds Mobile is an Android prediction-testing client built around fictional sports scenarios and abstract real-time 3D playback.

What it currently delivers:

- Synthetic football, basketball, and baseball scenarios
- Pre-match prediction lane selection
- Live 3D OpenGL ES simulation with anonymous standee actors
- Coaching interactions and reflex-based QTE prompts
- On-screen momentum, control, reflex, pressure, and prediction-edge feedback
- API 35 Android release build

What it intentionally avoids:

- No real teams, leagues, players, badges, likenesses, or franchise-sensitive identities
- No live regulated betting or wagering workflows
- No claim of guaranteed accuracy or production authorization

Why this build matters:

- It is suitable for client review, interaction testing, and early prediction experience evaluation while staying clear of real identity-sensitive sports assets.
