Runtime use in the current ROM:
- Title screen prefers title_menu_sheet when available.
- Tutorial overlays on the currently selected quadrant background.
- Real-time gameplay now happens directly on the top-down quadrant maps instead of in separate battle scenes.
- Tommy and Mareaou render from generated top-down-oriented character frames instead of placeholder primitives.
- Environmental objects and roaming critters are drawn from the generated environment sheets to populate the landscape.
- Combat and ambience overlays use the generated FX sheets during live map exploration.
- Combat HUD uses tommy_hud_sheet as the top and bottom panel art.
- Current special selection uses special_icon_sheet.
- Game-over and win screens prefer ingame_menu_and_endscreens_sheet when available.
- The gameplay renderer avoids full-screen redraws during exploration and instead restores small background regions behind moving actors to reduce visible flicker.
